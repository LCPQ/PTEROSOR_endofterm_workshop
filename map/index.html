Location

The conference will be held in the Auditorium of Le Village near the city center of Toulouse (local contact Tallulah Finch). The address is 31 Allée Jules Guesde 31000 TOULOUSE.







Toulouse is an amazing city with many monuments to visit and food to taste (like cassoulet!). For more info, visit Tourism in Toulouse. Here is a selection of monuments you do not want to miss. Note that there are all within walking distance from the workshop venue:

  • Capitole

  • Basilica of Saint-Sernin

  • Jacobins Convent

  • Hôtel d’Assézat

  • Canal du Midi

  • Jardin des plantes (very close to the workshop venue)


There are also plenty of sightseeing to do around Toulouse. Here is a non-exhaustive list:

  • Carcasonne, its castel and its famous cassoulet! (1h by train from Toulouse main station)

  • Albi and its cathedral (1h by train from Toulouse main station)

  • Cathar castels
